Twenty One Pilots

Twenty One Pilots (stylized in all lowercase or as twenty øne piløts) is an American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio. The band was formed in 2009 by lead vocalist Tyler Joseph along with Nick Thomas and Chris Salih, both of whom left in 2011.

Genres:

Alternative Rap, Electropop, Indie Pop, Pop Rock, Rap Rock, Alternative Rock

Members:

Tyler Joseph, Josh Dun, Nick Thomas, Chris Salih
